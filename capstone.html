Fanshawe IDP Capstone

Brothers In Arms

In the 4th term of the IDP 2025 program, students collaborated to create a website for Brothers In Arms, an organization dedicated to supporting veterans. By combining their diverse skills—design, development, and content creation—they worked together to produce a site that effectively communicates the organization's mission.
